US GRAIN CARLOADINGS FALL IN WEEK
  US grain carloadings totaled 25744
  cars in the week ended March 28 down 43 pct from the previous
  week but 416 pct above the corresponding week a year ago the
  Association of American Railroads reported
      Grain mill product loadings in the week totalled 10920
  cars up 01 pct from the previous week and 127 pct above the
  same week a year earlier the association said
