MAURITIAN SUGAR EXPORTS FALL IN FEBRUARY
  Mauritius exported 47144 tonnes of
  sugar tel quel in February down from 57911 tonnes tel
  quel shipped in January and 89351 in February 1986 the
  Mauritius Chamber of Agricultures Sugar News Bulletin said
      Opening stocks at the beginning of last month totalled
  320057 tonnes against 288406 tonnes at the start of February
  last year Closing stocks at the end of February were 270212
  tonnes up from 196309 tonnes at endFebruary 1986
      The estimate for 1986 sugar production was unchanged from
  last month at 748472 tonnes raw value the Chamber said
