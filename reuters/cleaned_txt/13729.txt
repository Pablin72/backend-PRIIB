UK INTERVENTION WHEAT SOLD TO HOME MARKET
  A total of 14685 tonnes of British
  intervention feed wheat were sold at todays tender for the
  home market the Home Grown Cereals Authority said
      Bids amounted to 19115 tonnes against offerings of 31528
  Prices paid were at or above the prevailing intervention feed
  wheat price of 12071 stg per tonne
      Grain traders said a large part of the tonnage on offer was
  in stores which would mean high transport costs to deficient
  areas The European Commission recently made available 70000
  tonnes for next weeks tender
