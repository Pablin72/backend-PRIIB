UK MONEY MARKET GIVEN 689 MLN STG EARLY HELP
  The Bank of England said it had provided
  the money market with early assistance of 689 mln stg in
  response to an early round of bill offers from the discount
  houses This compares with the Banks estimate that the system
  would face a shortage of around 12 billion stg today
      The central bank made outright purchases of bank bills
  comprising 347 mln stg in band one at 978 pct 207 mln stg in
  band two at 91316 pct and 135 mln stg in band three at 934
  pct
