UK GRAIN EXPORTS CONTINUE TO RISE SHARPLY
  The UK Exported 517600 tonnes of wheat
  and 315800 tonnes of barley in the first 25 days of March the
  Home Grown Cereals Authority HGCA said quoting provisional
  Customs and Excise figures
      This brought combined wheat and barley exports since the
  season started on July 1 to 760 mln tonnes substantially up
  on the 402 mln exported in the same 198586 period
      This seasons total comprises 394 mln tonnes of wheat and
  366 mln barley compared with 163 mln and 239 mln
  respectively a year ago
