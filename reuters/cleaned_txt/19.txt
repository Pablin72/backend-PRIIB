BONUS WHEAT FLOUR FOR NORTH YEMEN   USDA
  The Commodity Credit Corporation CCC
  has accepted an export bonus offer to cover the sale of 37000
  long tons of wheat flour to North Yemen the US Agriculture
  Department said
      The wheat four is for shipment MarchMay and the bonus
  awarded was 11905 dlrs per tonnes and will be paid in the form
  of commodities from the CCC inventory
      The bonus was awarded to the Pillsbury Company
      The wheat flour purchases complete the Export Enhancement
  Program initiative announced in April 1986 it said
