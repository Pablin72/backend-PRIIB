JAPANS 198687 SUGAR CANE OUTPUT SEEN FALLING
  An Agriculture Ministry survey has
  estimated the nations 198687 crop sugar cane output at 214
  mln tonnes down from 262 mln a year earlier
      Ministry officials said the decline was due to bad weather
  and a reduction in the land under sugar cane this season which
  totals 34800 hectares down from 35700 in 198586 they said
      Harvesting stretches from December 1986 to April 1987
      Japans final output will be announced at the beginning of
  June they said
