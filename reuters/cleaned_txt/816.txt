US BRASS MILL COPPER STOCKS LOWER IN JANUARY
  US brass mill copper stocks fell to
  185400 short tons copper content at the end of January from
  191200 short tons at the end of December according to the
  American Bureau of Metal Statistics
      Consumption by brass mills increased to 60700 short tons
  in January from 48900 short tons in December
      Mills consumed 29600 tons of refinery shapes in January
  versus 24000 tons in December Scrap consumption increased to
  31100 tons in January from 24900 tons in December
      Total brass mill shipments increased to 69600 tons in
  January from 54400 tons in December while receipts rose to
  63800 tons from 47100 tons
