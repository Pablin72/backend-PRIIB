CHINA ADDS CANCELS WHEAT COMMITMENTS  USDA
  China has added 90000 tonnes of US
  wheat to its purchases for delivery in the 198788 season and
  cancelled 30000 tonnes of wheat purchases for delivery in the
  198687 season the US Agriculture Department said
      According to the departments Export Sales Report covering
  transactions in the week ended February 26 China has
  outstanding wheat commitments for the 198687 season of 30000
  tonnes and 420000 tonnes for delivery in the 198788 season
      The wheat season begins June 1
      China has total corn commitments for 198687 of 1011200
  tonnes and soybeans commitments of 157500 tonnes
      The season for corn and soybeans began September 1
