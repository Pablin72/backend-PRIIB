MALAYSIA RAISES PALM OIL EXPORT DUTIES
  The Malaysian government said it
  raised the export duty on processed palm oil ppo to 7090
  ringgit per tonne from 6406 in March effective today
      Export duty on crude palm oil cpo rose to 3830 ringgit
  per tonne from 1606 last month
      The gazetted price for ppo rose to 8196600 ringgit per
  tonne from 7968604 a month earlier and that on cpo to 7110037
  ringgit from 6178238
      The export duty and gazetted price of palm kernel stood
  unchanged at 19115 and 95575 ringgit per tonne respectively
