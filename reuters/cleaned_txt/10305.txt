USDA REJECTS SRI LANKAS 80 US DLR WHEAT PRICE
  Sri Lankan Food Department officials
  said the US Department of Agriculture rejected a US Firms
  offer of 80 US Dlrs per tonne CAF to supply 52500 tonnes of
  soft wheat to Colombo from the Pacific Northwest
      They said Sri Lankas Food Department subsequently made a
  counteroffer to five US Firms to buy wheat at 85 US Dlrs
  CAF for April 816 delivery
      The company which obtains USDA approval for the proposed
  price must inform the Department before 1330 gmt they said
