US M1 MONEY SUPPLY RISES 500 MLN DLRS
  US M1 money supply rose 500 mln
  dlrs to a seasonally adjusted 7387 billion dlrs in the March 9
  week the Federal Reserve said
      The previous weeks M1 level was revised to 7382 billion
  dlrs from 7380 billion while the fourweek moving average of
  M1 rose to 7382 billion dlrs from 7372 billion
      Economists polled by Reuters said that M1 would be
  anywhere from unchanged to up five billion dlrs The average
  forecast called for a 23 billion dlr increase
