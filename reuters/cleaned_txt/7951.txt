CCC GUARANTEES TO TURKEY SWITCHED TO MEALSUSDA
  The Commodity Credit Corporation
  CCC has reallocated 150 mln dlrs in credit guarantees to
  Turkey previously earmarked for sales of US oilseeds to
  provide coverage for sales of US protein meals in fiscal year
  1987 the US Agriculture Department said
      The department said the action reduces the guarantee line
  authorized for sales of oilseeds to 50 mln dlrs from 200 mln
  dlrs and creates the new line for protein meals
      All sales under the credit lines must be registered and
  exports completed by September 30 1987 it said
