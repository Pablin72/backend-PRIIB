PACIFIC RESOURCES ltPRI INSTALLS OIL MOORING
  Pacific Resources Inc said it has
  installed a CALM Catenary Anchor Leg Mooring singlepoint
  mooring terminal off the southwest coast of Oahu at its
  Hawaiian Independent Refinery at a cost of 35 mln dlrs
      The system transfers crude and fuel oils from tankers to
  the refinerys tank farm and carries refined products to ships
  for export PRI said
      Company chairman Robert G Reed said the new mooring system
  will permit 24hour service in most kinds of weather and will
  reduce ship turnaround time He said the mooring is the first
  of its kind in the US
      The new system can accomodate vessels up to 150000
  deadweight tons or one mln barrels of cargo PRI said
