NORWAYS WHOLESALE PRICES RISE 05 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  Norways wholesale price index base 1981
  rose 05 pct in February to 1360 after a 17 pct rise in
  January to 1350 the Central Bureau of Statistics said
      The year on year increase for February was 79 pct compared
  with 57 pct in January and 22 pct in February 1986 it added
