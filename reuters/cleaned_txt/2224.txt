INVESTMENT FIRM BOOSTS PUROLATOR ltPCC STAKE
  Halcyon Investments a New York
  investment partnership that deals mainly in risk arbitrage and
  stock trading said it raised its Purolator Courier Corp stake
  to 726700 shares or 95 pct from 474900 or 62 pct
      In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission
  Halcyon whose managing partner is Alan B Slifka and Co said
  it bought 201800 Purolator common shares between Feb 3 and
  March 2 at prices ranging from 28689 to 34811 dlrs each
      Halcyon which said it has spent 201 mln dlrs for its
  Purolator common shares said it also acquired options on Feb
  27 giving it the right to buy 50000 shares for 18 mln dlrs
