NEW ZEALAND UNEMPLOYMENT FALLS IN FEBRUARY
  New Zealands unemployment rate fell
  to 60 pct of the workforce at the end of February from 61 pct
  in January but was above the 43 pct level of February 1986
  the labour department said
      It said the number of unemployed fell to 78711 from 81558
  in January and compared with 57103 in February 1986
