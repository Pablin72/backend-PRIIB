JAMAICA AUTHORIZED TO BUY PL 480 WHEAT  USDA
  Jamaica has been authorized to
  purchase about 56000 tonnes of US wheat under an existing PL
  480 agreement the US Agriculture Department said
      It may buy the wheat valued at 70 mln dlrs between March
  25 and August 341 and ship it from US ports andor Canadian
  transshipment points by September 30 1987
