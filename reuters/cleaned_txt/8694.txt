PAKISTAN CONSUMER PRICE INDEX FALLS IN JANUARY
  Pakistans consumer price index base
  197576 fell to 23193 in January 1987 from 23326 in December
  1986 and compared with  22366 a year ago the federal Bureau
  of Statistics said
      The wholesale price index same base rose to 22983 in
  January from 22906 in December and compared with  21797 in
  January 1986
