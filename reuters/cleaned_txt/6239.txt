USDA REPORTS 350000 TONNES CORN TO UNKNOWN
  The US Agriculture Department said
  private US exporters reported sales of 350000 tonnes of corn
  for delivery to unknown destinations during the 198687
  marketing season
      The marketing year for corn began September 1
      This is the second day running that exporters have reported
  corn sales to unknown destinations Yesterday they reported
  sales of 150000 tonnes to unknown
