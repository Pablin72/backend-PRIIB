UK MONEY MARKET GIVEN FURTHER 168 MLN STG HELP
  The Bank of England said it had provided
  the money market with a further 168 mln stg assistance in the
  afternoon session This takes the Banks total help so far
  today to 188 billion stg and compares with its estimate of a
  record two billon stg shortage in the system
      The central bank purchased bank bills outright comprising
  25 mln stg in band one at 978 pct 138 mln stg in band two at
  91316 pct and three mln stg in band three at 934 pct
      It also bought two mln stg of treasury bills in band two at
  91316 pct
