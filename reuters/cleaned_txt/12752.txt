DANISH UNEMPLOYMENT FALLS TO 78 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  Denmarks seasonally adjusted
  unemployment rate fell to 78 pct of the workforce in February
  from 79 pct in January against 79 pct in February 1986 the
  National Statistics Office said
      The total number of unemployed in February was 213200
  against 215700 in January and 216600 in February 1986
