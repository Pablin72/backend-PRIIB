THAI RICE EXPORTS FALL IN WEEK TO MARCH 31
  Thailand exported 75160 tonnes of rice
  in the week ended March 31 down from 88785 tonnes the
  previous week the Commerce Ministry said
      It said the government and private exporters shipped 36552
  and 38608 tonnes respectively
      Private exporters concluded advance weekly sales for 22086
  tonnes against 44483 tonnes the previous week
      Thailand exported 123 mln tonnes of rice in JanuaryMarch
  down from 129 mln tonnes a year ago It has commitments to
  export another 381171 tonnes this year
