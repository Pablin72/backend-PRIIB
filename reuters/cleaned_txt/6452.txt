UK MONEY MARKET GIVEN 15 MLN STG AFTERNOON HELP
  The Bank of England said it had provided
  the money market with a further 15 mln stg assistance This
  brings the Banks total help so far today to 344 mln stg and
  compares with the estimated shortage of around 13 billion stg
      The central bank purchased bank bills outright at the
  lower dealing rates established this morning comprising one
  mln stg in band one at 978 pct and 14 mln stg in band two at
  91316 pct
