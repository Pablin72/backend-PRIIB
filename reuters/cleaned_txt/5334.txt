PATON REPORTS US GREEN COFFEE ROASTINGS HIGHER
  US roastings of green coffee in the
  week ended March 7 were about 325000 60kilo bags including
  that used for soluble production compared with 290000 bags in
  the corresponding week of last year and about 315000 bags in
  the week ended February 28 George Gordon Paton and Co Inc
  reported
      It said cumulative roastings for calendar 1987 now total a
