FED EXPECTED TO ADD TEMPORARY RESERVES
  The Federal Reserve is expected to
  enter the US Government securities market to add temporary
  reserves economists said
      They expect it to supply the reserves indirectly by
  arranging 15 to two billion dlrs of customer repurchase
  agreements The Fed may add the reserves directly instead via
  System repurchases
      Federal funds which averaged 618 pct yesterday opened at
  6316 pct and stayed there in early trading Analysts said the
  rate is under upward pressure partly from settlement of 825
  billion dlrs of fiveyear Treasury notes
