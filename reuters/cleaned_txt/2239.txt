KAINES SELLS JORDAN TWO CARGOES OF WHITE SUGAR
  Trade house Kaines said it sold Jordan
  two cargoes of white sugar at its buying tender today
      The sale comprised two 12000 to 14000 tonne cargoes plus
  or minus 10 pct for MarApr shipment a Kaines trader said
      Traders said the business was done at 2355 dlrs a tonne
  cost and freight
