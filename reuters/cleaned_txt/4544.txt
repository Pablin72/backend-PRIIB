US M1 MONEY SUPPLY FALLS 600 MLN DLRS
  US M1 money supply fell 600 mln
  dlrs to a seasonally adjusted 7380 billion dlrs in the March 2
  week the Federal Reserve said
      The previous weeks M1 level was revised to 7386 billion
  dlrs from 7385 billion while the fourweek moving average of
  M1 rose to 7371 billion dlrs from 7368 billion
      Commenting on February growth of the broader monetary
  aggregates the Fed said that M2 fell 16 billion dlrs while
  M3 rose three billion Economists polled by Reuters had
  projected a 900 mln dlr fall in M1 a 18 billion dlr drop in
  M2 and no change in M3
