SOVIET SUGAR IMPORTS HIGHER IN OCTNOV
  Soviet sugar imports in October and
  November were significantly higher than in the same period of
  the year before according to figures received by the
  International Sugar Organization
      Imports in October totalled 23803 tonnes compared with
  4685 tonnes in the same month of 1985 while November imports
  were up to 136029 tonnes from 46541
      For the first 11 months of 1986 Soviet imports totalled
  512 mln tonnes against 430 mln in the same period of 1985
      The October 1986 import figure consisted entirely of whites
  from Cuba while the November total was made up of 84037
  tonnes Cuban whites and 51992 tonnes whites from Thailand Of
  the imports in the JanuaryNovember period those from Cuba
  were up to 381 mln tonnes from 365 mln and from Thailand to
  292808 tonnes from 22800
      Soviet exports also increased in 1986 The JanuaryNovember
  export total of 289232 compares with 165859 tonnes in the
  first 11 months of 1985 Exports in October 1986 were 20064
  down from 38853 a year earlier while November exports were up
  from 32796 to 50855 tonnes
