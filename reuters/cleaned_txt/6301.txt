API SAYS DISTILLATE GAS STOCKS OFF IN WEEK
  Distillate fuel stocks held in
  primary storage fell by 735 mln barrels in the week ended
  March 13 to 11274 mln barrels from a revised 12009 mln the
  previous week the American Petroleum Institute API said
      In its weekly statistical bulletin the oil industry trade
  group said gasoline stocks fell 289 mln barrels to 24844 mln
  barrels from a revised 25133 mln and crude oil stocks dropped
  439 mln barrels to 32513 mln from a revised 32952 mln
      It said residual fuel stocks fell 250000 barrels to 3573
  mln from 3598 mln barrels
      API said refinery runs in the week rose to 1180 mln
  barrels per day bpd from a revised 1170 mln and refinery use
  of operating capacity was 757 pct up from a revised 750 pct
      Crude oil imports in the week rose to 366 mln bpd from a
  revised 267 mln API added
