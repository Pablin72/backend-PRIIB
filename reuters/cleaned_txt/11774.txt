BANK OF JAPAN INTERVENES IN EARLY TOKYO AFTERNOON
  The Bank of Japan intervened in the market
  in the early afternoon buying dollars around 14730 yen and
  continuing to buy them as high as 14750 yen dealers said
      The Bank intervened just after the dollar started rising on
  buying by securities houses at around 14705 yen and hoped to
  accelerate the dollars advance they said
      The dollar rose as high as 14750 yen
