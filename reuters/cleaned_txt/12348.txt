MEXICO BUYS 51000 TONNES US SOYBEANS
  Mexico bought 51000 tonnes of US
  number two yellow soybeans in an overnight tender for various
  ocean and rail shipments in May private export sources said
      About 40000 tonnes sold for ocean shipment at prices
  ranging from 19967 to 21017 dlrs per tonne c and f and
  about 11000 tonnes sold for rail shipment at prices from
  20550 to 21788 dlrs per tonne they said
