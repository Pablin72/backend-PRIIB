EGYPT AUTHORIZED TO BUY PL 480 WHEAT FLOURUSDA
  Egypt has been authorized to purchase
  about 125000 tonnes of US wheat flour under an existing PL
  480 agreement the US Agriculture Department said
      It may buy the wheat flour valued at 230 mln dlrs between
  April 8 and August 31 1987 and ship it by September 30 the
  department said
