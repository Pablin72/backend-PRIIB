NORWAYS TRADE DEFICIT WIDENS IN FEBRUARY
  Norways trade deficit widened in February
  to 957 mln crowns from 80 mln crowns in January and 492 mln
  crowns in February last year the Central Bureau of Statistics
  said
      Exports dropped to 1066 billion crowns last month
  compared with 1111 billion in January and 1085 billion in
  February 1986 it added
      Crude oil and natural gas exports totalled 456 billion
  crowns in February against 510 billion in January and 536
  billion a year ago
