YORK BUSINESS LOANS FALL 1.31 BILLION DLRS Commercial industrial loans books 10 major York banks, excluding acceptances, fell 1.31 billion dlrs 62.70 billion week ended April 1, Federal Reserve Bank York said. Including acceptances, loans dropped 1.34 billion dlrs 63.23 billion. Commercial paper outstanding nationally fell 4.80 billion dlrs 334.28 billion. National business loan data scheduled released Friday.
