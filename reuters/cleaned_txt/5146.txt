SWEDISH TRADE SURPLUS RISES IN FEBRUARY
  Swedens trade surplus rose to 36
  billion crowns in February from 15 billion in January and 348
  billion in February 1986 the Central Bureau of Statistics
  said
      The trade surplus for the first two months of the year rose
  to 51 billion crowns from 49 billion in the corresponding
  period of 1986
      The report said February imports stood at 201 billion
  crowns while exports were 237 billion
