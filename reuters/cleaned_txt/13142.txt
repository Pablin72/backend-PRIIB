PETROBRAS SEES RAISING EXPORT OF FUEL IN 1987
  Brazils state oil company
  Petrobras is pledging to export 46 mln cubic meters of fuel
  or 289 mln barrels in 1987 a company spokesman said
      He said that represents a total sale worth 600 mln dlrs
  The volume is 27 pct higher over 1986 sales which totalled 36
  mln cubic meters or 226 mln barrels
      The United States Africa and Latin American are Brazils
  main fuel buyers the spokesman said
