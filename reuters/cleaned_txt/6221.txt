HONDURAS AUTHORIZED TO BUY PL 480 COMMODITIES
  Honduras has been authorized to buy
  about 75000 tonnes of US wheat about 15000 tonnes of US
  corn and about 6000 tonnes of US tallow under an existing
  PL 480 agreement the US Agriculture Department said
      The department said it may buy the wheat valued at 85 mln
  dlrs the corn valued at 15 mln and the tallow valued at
  20 mln dlrs between March 24 and August 31 1987 and ship it
  from US ports andor Canadian transshipment points by this
  September 30
      The purchase authorizations cover the entire quantity
  provided under the agreement signed March 11
