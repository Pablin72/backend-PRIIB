PERCEPTION TECHNOLOGY ltPCEP TO TAKE CHARGE
  Perception Technology Corp said
  it expects to take a charge of about 686000 dlrs or 19 cts per

  due to the bankruptcy proceeding of customer TC of New York
  Inc

  TC of about 2480000 dlrs

  arrangements that might be made with the bankruptcy court on
  the equipment
