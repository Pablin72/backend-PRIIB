COLOMBIAN INFLATION RISES 203 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  colombias cost of living index rose 203
  pct in february after a 327 pct increase in january and a 315
  pct rise in february 1986 the government statistics institute
  said
      The rise brought yearonyear inflation to 1977 pct
  compared with 2372 pct at endfebruary 1986 and 2166 pct for
  the year ending january 1987
      The government has predicted that inflation this year would
  be lower than in 1986 when it reached 209 pct
