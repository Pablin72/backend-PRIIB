ST.GOBAIN UNIT ISSUES ECU BOND GOLD WARRANTS St.Gobain Netherlands, guaranteed Cie de St.Gobain, issuing 75 mln ECU bond gold warrants attached, due 6, 1992 carrying 4-1/2 pct coupon priced par, lead manager Salomon Brothers International said. Fees comprise 1-1/4 pct selling concession 5/8 pct management underwriting combined. Listing Luxembourg. 1,000 ECU bond carries gold warrant exercisable 6, 1987 6, 1990 entitling holder purchase ounce exercise price 490 dlrs.
