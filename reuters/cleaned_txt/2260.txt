PRUDENTIAL INSURANCE YEAR EARNINGS
  The Prudential Insurance Company of
  America a privately held company said today that net income
  in 1986 rose to 28 billion dlrs from the 23 billion dlrs
  reported the year earlier
      Assets under management rose to 1775 billion dlrs in 1986
  from 1501 billion dlrs in 1985 while consolidated assets
  jumped to 1345 billion dlrs from 1157 billion dlrs
      Discussing its major subsidiaries the company said that
  Prudential Capital and Investment Services Inc the holding
  company for brokerage house PrudentialBache Securities and
  certain other related subsidiaries earned 142 million dlrs in
  1986 Of that the securities operations of PrudentialBache
  netted 817 mln dlrs after taxes and a charge of 25 mln dlrs
