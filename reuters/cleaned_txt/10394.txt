ABIDJAN PORT ACTIVITY RISES
  The tonnage of goods passing through
  Ivory Coasts main port of Abidjan rose 23 pct last year
  according to the Ivorian Chamber of Commerce
      Its monthly report said 947 mln tonnes of goods passed
  through the port last year compared with 926 mln the year
  before Exports fell to 375 mln from 389 mln tonnes while
  imports rose to 572 mln from 537 mln
