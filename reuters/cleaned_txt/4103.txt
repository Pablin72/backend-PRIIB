INDIA 198687 COTTON EXPORT QUOTA UP 190000 BALES
  Indias raw cotton export quota has
  been raised by 190000 170kg bales to 600000 bales in 198687
  ending August still well below the 198586 quota of 135 mln
  bales Minister of State for Textiles RN Mirdha said
      State and private agencies contracted to export 134 mln
  bales in 198586 he told journalists But only 433000 bales
  were shipped that year with the rest to be delivered in
  198687 About 758000 bales from 198586 contracts were
  shipped up to February 2 in 198687
       The government will export 600000 bales of long and
  extra long staple cotton in the three years from 198687 he
  said
