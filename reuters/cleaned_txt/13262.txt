NATIONAL AUSTRALIA BANK LOWERS BENCHMARK PRIME
  National Australia Bank Ltd ltNABAS
  said it lowered its benchmark prime lending rate to 1825 pct
  from 185 effective today but left its base rate at 185
      The benchmark reduction brings the rate into line with the
  prime rates of most of Australias trading banks including
  those of two of the other three major trading banks
      However the rate is above the 18 pct  the lowest ruling
  rate  set by the other major the Australia and New Zealand
  Banking Group Ltd ltANZAS on Friday and effective today
      The benchmark is based on shortterm interest rate
  movements while the base rate is tied to longerterm trends
