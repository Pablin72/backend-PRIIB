USDA ESTIMATES CANADIAN CROPS U.S. Agriculture Department estimated Canada's 1986/87 wheat crop 31.85 mln tonnes, 31.85 mln tonnes month. estimated 1985/86 output 24.25 mln tonnes, 24.25 mln month. Canadian 1986/87 coarse grain production projected 27.62 mln tonnes, 27.62 mln tonnes month. Production 1985/86 estimated 24.95 mln tonnes, 24.95 mln month. Canadian wheat exports 1986/87 forecast 19.00 mln tonnes, 19.00 mln tonnes month. Exports 1985/86 estimated 17.72 mln tonnes, 17.71 mln month.
