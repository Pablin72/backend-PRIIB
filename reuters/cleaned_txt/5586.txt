CANBRA FOODS SETS SPECIAL FIVE DLRSHR PAYOUT
  ltCanbra Foods Ltd earlier
  reporting a 1986 net profit against a yearago loss said it
  declared a special onetime dividend of five dlrs per common
  share pay March 31 record March 26
      Canbra said it set the special payout to allow shareholders
  to participate in the gain on the sale of unit Stafford Foods
  Ltd in November 1986 as well as the companys unusually
  profitable performance in 1986
      Canbra earlier reported 1986 net earnings of 42 mln dlrs
  excluding a 13 mln dlr gain on the Stafford sale compared to
  a yearago loss of 15 mln dlrs
