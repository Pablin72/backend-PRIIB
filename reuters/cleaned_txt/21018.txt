STATOIL AWARDS VESLEFRIKK OIL FIELD CONTRACTS Norwegian state oil company Den Norske Stats Oljeselskap (Statoil) signed contracts worth total 1.5 billion Norwegian crowns connection development Veslefrikk oil field, Statoil said. Moss Rosenberg Verft Stavanger awarded contract convert drilling platform West Vision floating production platform. work completed summer 1989. Aker Verdal awarded contract engineering, purchasing construction steel jacket wellhead platform, completed 1989, Statoil said.
