UK MONEY MARKET GIVEN FURTHER 442 MLN STG HELP
  The Bank of England said it provided the
  market with a further 442 mln stg assistance during the morning
  to offset a liquidity shortage it estimated at 160 billion
  stg revised up from 155 billion
      The bank bought outright eight mln stg of band one treasury
  bills and five mln stg of band one bank bills at 1038 pct
      It bought a further 429 mln stg of bills for resale to the
  discount houses on April 2 at an interest rate of 10716 pct
      So far today the bank has given the market assistance
  worth a total of 1501 billion stg
