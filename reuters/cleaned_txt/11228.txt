UK MONEY MARKET GIVEN FURTHER 570 MLN STG HELP
  The Bank of England said it provided the
  market with a further 570 mln stg assistance during the morning
  after revising its estimate of the liquidity shortage to 185
  billion stg before taking account of its early round of bill
  purchases
      Initially the Bank put the likely shortage at some 175
  billion and to help offset this gave early assistance of 1143
  billion
      Its total help so far today amounts to 1713 billion stg
