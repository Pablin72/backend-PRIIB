EIA SAYS DISTILLATE GAS STOCKS OFF IN WEEK
  Distillate fuel stocks held in
  primary storage fell by 88 mln barrels in the week ended March
  six to 1196 mln barrels the Energy Information Administration
  EIA said
      In its weekly petroleum status report the Department of
  Energy agency said gasoline stocks were off 500000 barrels in
  the week to 2510 mln barrels and refinery crude oil stocks
  fell 12 mln barrels to 3318 mln
      The EIA said residual fuel stocks fell 15 mln barrels to
  364 mln barrels and crude oil stocks in the Strategic
  Petroleum Reserve SPR rose 200000 barrels to 5167 mln
      The total of all crude refined product and SPR stocks fell
  103 mln barrels to 15648 it said
