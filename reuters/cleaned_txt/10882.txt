TRADE SEES US CORN EXPORTS UP WHEATBEANS OFF
  Grain traders and analysts expect lower
  wheat and soybean exports and higher corn exports than a year
  ago in the USDAs export inspection report today
      Corn export guesses ranged from 270 mln to 320 mln
  bushels compared with the 276 mln inspected last week and
  205 mln a year ago
      Soybean export guesses ranged from 140 mln to 160 mln up
  from the 134 mln inspected last week but below the 255 mln
  reported a year ago
      Wheat estimates ranged from 110 mln to 140 mln bushels
  compared with 120 mln reported last week and 183 mln a year
  ago
