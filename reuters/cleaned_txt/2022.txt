UK MONEY MARKET GIVEN HELP OFFERED FACILITIES
  The Bank of England said it had given the
  money market 206 mln stg of assistance this afternoon and
  offered the discount houses borrowing facilities to take out
  the rest of the 110 billion stg shortage revised down from an
  initial 115 billion estimate
      It made no alteration to its established dealing rates
  buying 95 mln stg of band one bank bills at 1078 pct and 111
  mln of band two bank bills at 101316 pct This brings the
  Banks total assistance so far today to 243 mln stg
