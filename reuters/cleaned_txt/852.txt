  Hartford Insurance Group a unit
  of New Yorkbased ITT Corp said higher worldwide premiums help
  boost net income for the 1986 fourth quarter to 886 mln dlrs
  from net income of 367 mln dlrs for the 1985 quarter
     For the full year Hartford said it earned 329 mln dlrs up
  from 1514 mln dlrs in 1985
      Hartford said results for the year were aided by a gain of
  465 mln dlrs on the sale of its remaining 52 pct stake in
  Abbey Life Group PLC For 1985 the company posted a gain of
  143 mln dlrs on the sale of 48 pct of Abbey Life
      Hartford said total propertycasualty and lifehealth
  written premiums rose 25 pct in 1986 to eight billion dlrs
  from 64 billion dlrs in 1985
