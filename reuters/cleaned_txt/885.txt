MALAYSIA RAISES DUTY ON PROCESSED PALM OIL
  The government said it raised the
  export duty on processed palm oil PPO to 6406 ringgit per
  tonne from 4096 ringgit effective from March 1
      Export duty on crude palm oil CPO was unchanged at 1606
  ringgit per tonne
      The gazetted price of PPO rose to 7968604 ringgit per
  tonne from 7198286 That of CPO remained at 6178238 ringgit
      The export duty and gazetted price of palm kernel were left
  unchanged at 19115 and 95575 ringgit per tonne respectively
