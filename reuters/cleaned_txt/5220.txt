CHINA REPORTS 700 MLN DLR TWOMONTH TRADE DEFICIT
  Chinas trade deficit totalled 700 mln
  dlrs in the first two months of this year according to figures
  released by the State Statistics Bureau
      The New China News Agency quoted the Bureau as saying
  foreign trade totalled 93 billion dlrs in the period of which
  exports were worth 43 billion dlrs
      The bureau said total trade volume was up 25 pct on the
  same 1986 period with exports up 181 pct but it gave no
  other comparative figures
