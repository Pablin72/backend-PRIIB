ITALY CONSUMER PRICES RISE 04 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  Italys consumer price index rose 04 pct
  in February compared with January after rising 06 pct in
  January over December the national statistics institute
  Istat said
      The yearonyear rise in February was 42 pct down from 45
  pct in January and compared with 76 pct in February 1986
      Istat said its consumer prices index for the families of
  workers and employees base 1985 was 1091 in February against
  1087 in January and 1047 in February 1986
