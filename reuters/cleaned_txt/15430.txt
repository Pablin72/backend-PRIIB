CANADA FEBRUARY TRADE SURPLUS 1.2 BILLION DLRS Canada trade surplus 1.25 billion dlrs February compared upward revised 623 mln dlrs surplus January, Statistics Canada said. January surplus originally reported 533 mln dlrs. February surplus year 189 mln dlrs. February exports, seasonally adjusted, 10.44 billion dlrs 9.85 billion January 10.05 billion February, 1986. February imports 9.19 billion dlrs 9.23 billion January 9.86 billion February, 1986.
