GERMAN FEBRUARY IMPORT PRICES FALL
  Import prices in West Germany fell
  07 pct in February from January to stand 156 pct below their
  level in February 1986 the Federal Statistics Office said
      In January the import price index base 1980 was unchanged
  compared with December but 178 pct lower against January 1986
      February export prices same base as import prices were
  unchanged compared with January and 25 pct lower than in
  February 1986
      In January export prices fell 03 pct against December to
  stand 30 pct lower than in January 1986
