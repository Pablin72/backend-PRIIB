FRANCE SOLD WHEAT FLOUR TO CHINA  ONIC
  France has sold between 50000 to 100000
  tonnes of wheat flour to China the Director General of
  Frances Cereal Intervention Board ONIC Bernard Vieux said
      He gave no further details of the sale but added French
  millers were worried about the unfair competition facing French
  flour due the lack of endofseason storage premiums for wheat
      ONIC raised its estimate of 198687 flour exports to 170
  mln tonnes compared 165 mln forecast in March and the 187 mln
  exported in 198586
