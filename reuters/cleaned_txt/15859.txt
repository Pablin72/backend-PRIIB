USDA REVISES 1986 SUMMER POTATOES U.S. Agriculture Department made revisions 1986 crop summer potatoes -- Production -- 21,003,000 cwt (100 lbs), 20,900,000 cwt estimated previously. Acreage harvest -- 95,700 acres, 95,800 acres estimated previously. Yield harvested acre -- 219 cwt acre, 218 cwt acre previously estimated, department said.
