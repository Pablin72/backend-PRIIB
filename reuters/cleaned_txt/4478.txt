USDA SAYS WHEAT PURCHASED FROM FOREIGN SELLERS
  The US Agriculture Department said
  private US exporters reported purchases from foreign sellers
  of 120000 tonnes of wheat for delivery to unknown
  destinations
      The wheat consisting of 60000 tonnes of hard red winter
  and 60000 tones of soft red winter is for delivery during the
  198788 season which begins June 1
      A purchase from a foreign seller is a transaction in which
  a US exporter contracts to buy USproduced commodities from
  a foreign firm the department said
