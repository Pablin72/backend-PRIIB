UK MONEY MARKET RECEIVES 226 MLN STG ASSISTANCE
  The Bank of England said it operated in
  the money market this afternoon buying 226 mln stg in bills
      In band one the central bank bought 37 mln stg treasury
  bills and 72 mln stg bank bills at 978 pct together with 117
  mln stg band two bank bills at 91316 pct
      This brings total money market help so far today to 241 mln
  stg and compares with the Banks revised estimate of a 350 mln
  stg shortfall
