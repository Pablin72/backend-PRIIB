BELGOLUX TRADE SWINGS INTO JANUARY DEFICIT
  The BelgoLuxembourg Economic Union
  recorded a provisional trade deficit of 945 billion francs in
  January after a December surplus of 1532 billion francs
  revised upwards from a provisional surplus of 1194 billion
  the National Statistics Office said
      In January 1986 the union recorded a deficit of 2331
  billion francs
      January imports fell to a provisional 22886 billion francs
  from 24024 billion in December and 28165 billion a year
  earlier but exports were also lower at 21941 billion francs
  against 25556 billion and 25834 billion respectively
