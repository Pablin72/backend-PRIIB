SOUTH KOREA PLANS 1112 PCT BUDGET RISE IN 1988
  South Korea plans to increase the size of
  its budget in 1988 by 11 to 12 pct from this years 15596
  billion won Economic Planning Board officials said
      The proposed boost is based on a government forecast that
  gross national product gnp will grow by more than 75 pct and
  the gnp deflator by 35 pct in 1988 against targets of 80 pct
  and 35 pct respectively this year they said
      Details of the 1988 budget in which spending will match
  revenue have yet to be worked out the officials said
      The balanced budget in 1986 totalled 138005 billion won
