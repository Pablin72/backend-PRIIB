MORE INTERMEDIATE US WHEAT CREDITS FOR MOROCCO
  The Commodity Credit Corporation has
  approved an additional 450 mln dlrs under its interemdiate
  export credit guarantee program GSM103 for sales of US
  wheat to Morocco the US Agriculture Department said
      The action increases Moroccos cumulative fiscal year 1987
  program for wheat under GSM103 to 750 mln dlrs
      The credit terms extended for exports under the program
  must be in excess of three years but no more than seven years
  to eligible for credit coverage All sales under this line must
  be registered and exported by September 30 1987
      The department also said the guarantee line for sales of
  US wheat under the Export Credit Guarantee Program GSM102
  has been decreased 450 mln dlrs to 600 mln
