CHINA SWITCHES US WHEAT TO 198788 FROM 8687
  China has switched purchases of US
  wheat totaling 60000 tonnes from the 198687 season which
  began June 1 to 198788 season delivery the US Agriculture
  Department said
      The department said outstanding wheat sales to China for
  the current season amount to 90000 tonnes and sales for
  delivery in the 198788 season amount to 910000 tonnes
      Total corn commitments for the 198687 season total
  1015800 tonnes the department said
