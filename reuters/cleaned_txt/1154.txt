SWISS MONEY MARKET PAPER YIELDS 3286 PCT
  The Swiss Federal Governments new series
  of sixmonth money market certificates raised 1775 mln Swiss
  francs at an issue price of 98401 pct to give an average
  annual yield of 3286 pct the National Bank said
      Payment date is March 5
      The last series of sixmonth paper issued in January raised
  1596 mln francs at 98392 pct to give an average yield of
  3251 pct
