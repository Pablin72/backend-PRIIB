JAPAN CONSUMER PRICES UNCHANGED IN FEBRUARY
  Japans consumer price index base 1985
  was unchanged at 997 in February from a month earlier the
  governments Management and Coodination Agency said
      The index showed a 04 pct drop in January
      The February index was down one pct from a year earlier for
  the third consecutive yearonyear drop
      In January the index fell 11 pct from a year earlier the
  first drop of over one pct since a 13 pct drop in September
  1958
      In February petrol prices increased but winter clothing
  prices stayed low and vegetable prices fell
      The February year on year fall was due to lower vegetable
  fuel oil petrol electricity and gas prices and despite
  higher housing education footwear and clothing costs
      The unadjusted consumer price index for the Tokyo area
  base 1985 in midMarch rose 04 pct from a month earlier to
  1006 reflecting higher vegetable prices
      The index fell 03 pct year on year the third consecutive
  yearly drop reflecting lower food and utility costs
