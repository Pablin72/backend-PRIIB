JAPAN MARCH INTERIM TRADE SURPLUS FALLS
  Japans customscleared trade surplus
  fell to 189 billion dlrs in the first 10 days of March from a
  191 billion surplus a year earlier the Ministry of Finance
  said
       The March interim surplus compared with a 170 billion dlr
  surplus in the same February period
       FOB exports in the first 10 days of March rose 58 pct
  from a year earlier to 550 billion dlrs and CIF imports rose
  97 pct to 361 billion
       The average yendollar rate used for the statistics was
  15367 yen against 18123 a year earlier
