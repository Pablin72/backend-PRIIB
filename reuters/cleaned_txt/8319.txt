ROTTERDAM GRAIN HANDLER SAYS PORT BALANCE ROSE
  Graan Elevator Mij GEM said its
  balance in port of grains oilseeds and derivatives rose to
  70000 tonnes on March 21 compared with 10000 a week earlier
  after arrivals of 192000 tonnes and discharges of 132000
  tonnes last week
      The balance comprised 25000 tonnes of grains plus oilseeds
  and 45000 tonnes of derivatives
      This weeks estimated arrivals total 487000 tonnes of
  which 107000 are grainsoilseeds and 380000 derivatives
      The figures cover around 95 pct of Rotterdam traffic in the
  products concerned
