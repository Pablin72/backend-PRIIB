DANISH RESERVES RISE IN FEBRUARY
  Denmarks net official reserves rose
  to 3634 billion crowns in February from 2800 billion in
  January against a revised 4585 billion in February 1986 the
  central bank said in its monthly balance sheet report
      Total net reserves including reserves held by commercial
  and major savings banks rose to 3826 billion crowns from
  3011 billion in January compared with a revised 3599 billion
  in February last year
      The bank said provisional figures showed net registered
  private and public capital imports of 103 billion crowns in
  February
