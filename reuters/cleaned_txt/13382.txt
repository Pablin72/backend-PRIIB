JAPANS LIPC TO BUY BEEF ON APRIL 23
  Japans Livestock Industry Promotion Corp
  LIPC said that on April 28 it will import 7515 tonnes of
  beef for the first half of the 1987 fiscal year started on
  April 1 under the simultaneous buy and sell tender system
  against 6813 a year ago
      The system calls on the agency to buy beef from trading
  houses and simultaneously sell it to users in one tender The
  tender quota in AprilSeptember is 10 pct of the agencys beef
  import share Japans beef import quota in 198788 was set at
  93000 tonnes against 85000 for the same period in 198687
