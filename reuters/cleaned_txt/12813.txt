DUTCH COCOA BEAN IMPORTS RISE IN JANUARY
  Total Dutch imports of cocoa beans
  rose to 17978 tonnes in January from 13961 in January 1986
  while exports fell to 1852 tonnes from 3111 the Central
  Bureau of Statistics said
      Cocoa butter imports rose slightly to 1699 tonnes from
  1507 while exports fell slightly to 6211 from 6293 tonnes
      Imports of cocoa powder fell to 316 tonnes from 469 and
  exports to 5944 from 6106 tonnes
