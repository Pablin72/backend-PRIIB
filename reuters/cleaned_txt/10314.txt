BELGIAN DECEMBER INDUSTRIAL OUTPUT FALLS
  Industrial production excluding
  construction and adjusted for the number of working days fell
  15 pct in December from year earlier levels the National
  Statistics Office said
      It was also a sharp 168 pct below the November level it
  said
      The office said the production index base 1980 stood at
  973 in December against an adjusted 1169 in November and 988
  in December 1985
