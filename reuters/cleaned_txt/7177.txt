UK REVISES RETAIL PRICE INDEX FROM FEBRUARY
  The UK Government tomorrow will
  release the first of its Retail Price Index RPI figures
  calculated on a revised group of components and rebased on
  January 1987 as previously announced the Employment
  Department said
      The index Britains measure of inflation is for February
  Earlier data will not be revised as there are no equivalent
  figures including the new components a spokesman said
      Previously the RPI base was January 1974
      Market forecasts centre on a 0405 pct monthly rise in
  February and a year on year rate about 40 pct The government
  forecasts annual inflation will be 40 pct at the end of 1987
