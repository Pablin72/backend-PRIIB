UK MONEY MARKET GETS 347 MLN STG AFTERNOON HELP
  The Bank of England said it has operated
  in the money market this afternoon buying back bills totalling
  347 mln stg This brings the total help so far today to 517 mln
  stg and compares with the Banks revised estimate of an 800 mln
  stg shortfall
      The central bank purchased in band one 20 mln stg at 978
  pct in band two 254 mln at 91316 pct in band three 66 mln
  at 934 pct and in band four seven mln stg at 91116 pct
