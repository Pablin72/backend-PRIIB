UK MONEY MARKET RECEIVES 170 MLN STG HELP
  The Bank of England said it has operated
  in the money market this morning purchasing 170 mln stg bank
  bills This compares with the Banks revised estimate of a 750
  mln stg shortfall In band two the Bank bought 72 mln stg at
  91316 pct in band three 52 mln at 934 pct and in band four
  46 mln at 91116 pct
