CHINAS FOREIGN EXCHANGE RESERVES FALL DURING 1986
  Chinas foreign exchange reserves
  totalled 10514 billion dlrs at end1986 up from 1037 billion
  at endSeptember but down from 119 billion at end1985
  according to central bank figures published by the New China
  News Agency
      The agency said 2072 billion dlrs of the reserves was held
  by the state treasury down from 226 billion at endSeptember
  while 8442 billion was held by the Bank of China up from 811
  billion
      Chinas outstanding foreign debts rose to 7572 billion at
  end1986 from 5067 billion at end1985 Gold reserves stood at
  1267 mln ounces unchanged over the year
