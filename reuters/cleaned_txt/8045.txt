MULTIMEDIA SEES YEAR END LOSS
  MultiMedia Barter Ltd said it expects
  to report a net loss of 820000 dlrs or 17 cts a share for the
  year ended December 31 compared to a loss of 553000 or 11 cts
  a share in the prior year
      The fourth quarter resulted in a net loss of 227000 or
  four cts compared to a loss of 330000 or six cts a shares last
  year
      It said it is currently in the process of restructuring by
  reducing expenses and streamlining operations and has cut
  expenses from 50000 dlrs to less than 15000 dlrs a month
