UK INTERVENTION FEED WHEAT TENDER RESULT AWAITED
  Grain traders said they were still
  awaiting results of yesterdays UK Intervention feed wheat
  tender for the home market
      The market sought to buy 340000 tonnes more than double
  the remaining 150000 tonnes available under the current
  tender However some of the tonnage included duplicate bids
  for supplies in the same stores
      Since the tenders started last July 861000 tonnes of
  British feed wheat have been sold back to the home market
