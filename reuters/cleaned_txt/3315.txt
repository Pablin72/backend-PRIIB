USDA ORANGE JUICE YIELD ESTIMATE
  The US Agriculture Department
  projected an average yield of 147 gallons of frozen
  concentrated orange juice per box 420 degree brix equivalent
  from Floridas 198687 crop
      That compares with 146 gallons per box previously and 138
  gallons per box from the 198586 crop
      The crop reporting board said the estimates for the 198687
  season are based on maturity and yields tests as of March 1
