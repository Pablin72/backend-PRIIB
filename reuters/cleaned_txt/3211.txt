US LEAD INVENTORIES RISE IN FEBRUARY  ABMS
  Lead stocks held by US refiners rose
  to 37295 short tons at the end of February from 34224 short
  tons revised higher at the end of January the American
  Bureau of Metal Statistics reported

  February from 38759 short tons in January
      Shipments from plants increased to 30467 short tons in
  February from 27041 short tons revised lower in January the
