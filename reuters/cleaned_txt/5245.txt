FINNISH INFLATION FALLS TO 35 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  Yearon year Finnish consumer prices
  fell to 35 pct in February 1986 from 37 pct in January and
  46 pct in February 1985 the Central Statistical Office said
      Consumer prices rose 03 pct in February after rises of one
  pct in January and 05 pct in February 1985
      The consumer price index base 1981 was 1427 in February
  1422 in January and 1378 in February 1986
