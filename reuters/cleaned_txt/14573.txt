TREASURY BALANCES AT FED ROSE ON APRIL 6
  Treasury balances at the Federal
  Reserve rose on April 6 to 4262 billion dlrs from 3876
  billion dlrs on the previous business day the Treasury said in
  its latest budget statement
      Balances in tax and loan note accounts fell to 4950
  billion dlrs from 5004 billion dlrs on the same respective
  days
      The Treasurys operating cash balance totaled 9212 billion
  dlrs on April 6 compared with 8880 billion dlrs on April 3
