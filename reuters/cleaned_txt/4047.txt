TAIWANS FIRST QUARTER MAIZE IMPORTS SEEN RISING
  Taiwans maize import commitments are
  expected to rise to 970000 tonnes in the first four months of
  1987 from 870000 tonnes a year earlier a spokesman for the
  Joint Committee of Maize Importers told Reuters
      He said more than 75 pct of the imports come from the US
  And the rest from South Africa
      The maize import target for calendar 1987 is set at well
  over 34 mln tonnes compared with an actual 307 mln in 1985
  he added
