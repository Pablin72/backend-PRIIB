NEWMONT MINING CORP REDUCES STAKE IN PEABODY
  Newmont Mining Corp said it completed
  the previously announced reduction of its share in ltPeabody
  Holding Co Inc to 4997 pct from 6147 pct
      Newmont realized 1168 mln dlrs in the transaction
      For 1986 Newmont reported net income of 742 mln dlrs
  Peabody the largest coal producer in the US had net income
  of 1008 mln dlrs on sales of 14 billion dlrs in 1986
      Newmont doubled its ownership in Peabody in January by
  purchasing 3074 pct interest held by the Williams Cos for 320
  mln dlrs
      The reduction resulted from completion of an earlier
  announced agreement by Peabody to sell a 1501 pct interest in
  the company to Eastern Gas and Fuel Associates in exchange for
  all the coal properties owned by Eastern
