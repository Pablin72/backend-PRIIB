CENERGY ltCRG REPORTS 4TH QTR NET PROFIT
  Cenergy Corp reported fourth quarter net
  income of 790000 dlrs or seven cts per share on revenues of
  77 mln dlrs
      For the year it reported a net loss of 65 mln dlrs or 70
  cts per share as a result of writedowns in the book value of
  its oil and gas properties in the first two quarters Revenues
  were 37 mln dlrs
      Following the companys fiscal year ended March 31 1985
  it changed to a calender year end
      For the nine months ended Dec 31 1985 it reported a loss
  of 634 mln dlrs or 654 dlrs per share on revenues of 476 mln
  dlrs which it said was a result of noncash writedowns of oil
  and gas properties
      For the year ended March 31 1985 Cenergy reported net
  income of 3705000 dlrs or 36 cts per share on revenues of 71
  mln dlrs
      The company said its reserves during the year fell to five
  mln barrels from 64 mln barrels of oil and to 601 bilion
  cubic feet of gas from 637 BCF It said these reserves did not
  disappear but are available to produce as prices recover
