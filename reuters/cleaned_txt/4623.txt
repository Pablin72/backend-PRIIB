IELS ACMEX UNIT TO BID FOR CHEETHAM
  ltIndustrial Equity Ltd IEL unit
  ltAcmex Holdings Ltd said it proposed to make a formal takeover
  offer for all the issued capital of ltCheetham Ltd a producer
  of salt animal feed and seeds
      Acmex said in a statement it would offer one share for
  every two Cheetham shares or 340 dlrs cash for each share
      Acmex said it was presently entitled to 51 mln Cheetham
  shares or 1685 pct of its 3027 mln issued shares
      The scrip offer values Cheetham at about 110 mln dlrs
  based on Acmexs current price of 720 dlrs and the cash offer
  at about 104 mln Cheetham last traded at 310 dlrs
