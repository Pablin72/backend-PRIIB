FAO APPROVES EMERGENCY FOOD AID FOR ETHIOPIA
  The United Nations Food and Agriculture
  Organization FAO said it approved emergency food aid worth
  more than 143 mln dlrs for drought victims in Ethiopia
      The aid will include 30000 tonnes of wheat and 1200
  tonnes of vegetable oil for farmers in the Wollo and Illubabor
  regions
      FAO said it has also approved more than 14 mln dlrs of
  food aid for 8000 families in Sri Lanka In addition 583225
  dlrs of aid will be made available to Malawi to feed 96700
  people displaced from Mozambique and a further 340200 dlrs for
  cyclone victims in Vanuatu in the South Pacific
