UK CREDIT BUSINESS FALLS IN JANUARY
  New credit advanced by finance houses
  retailers bank credit cards and other specialist providers of
  credit slipped to 266 billion stg in January from 278 billion
  in December  but remained close to the average level for
  1986s fourth quarter the Department of Trade and Industry
  said
      Of the January total 115 billion stg was advanced on bank
  credit cards

  January were 30 pct lower than in the previous three months
  Within this total lending to consumers fell by 60 pct and
  lending to businesses declined by 50 pct

  above the total three months earlier the department said
