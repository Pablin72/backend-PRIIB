AUSTRALIAN UNEMPLOYMENT EASES IN FEBRUARY
  Australias seasonallyadjusted
  unemployment rate eased to 82 pct of the estimated workforce
  in February from 83 pct in January compared with 79 pct a
  year earlier the Statistics Bureau said
      The number of unemployed declined to 632100 from 638300
  in January against 594500 in February 1986 it said
      But unadjusted the number of jobless rose to 699800 or
  91 pct of the workforce from 671400 or 89 pct in January and
  658500 or 87 pct a year earlier
