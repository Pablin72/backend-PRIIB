KUWAITI OIL EXPORTS AFFECTED GULF WAR Kuwait's oil exports affected seven-year Iran-Iraq war, Kuwait Oil Tanker Company (KOTC) Chairman Managing Director Abdul Fattah al-Bader told Kuwaiti newspaper. "Kuwait exploited opportunities continue exporting oil reduction," Al-Anbaa newspaper quoted saying. KOTC made profits mln dinars (seven mln dlrs) fiscal year ending June, predicted lower profits year due higher costs chartering operating vessels.
