US WEEKLY SOYBEAN CRUSH 21782929 BUSHELS
  Reporting members of the National
  Soybean Processors Association NSPA crushed 21782929
  bushels of soybeans in the week ended Feb 25 compared with
  22345718 bushels in the previous week and 16568000 in the
  yearago week the association said
      It said total crushing capacity for members was 25873904
  bushels vs 25873904 last week and 25459238 bushels last
  year
      NSPA also said US soybean meal exports in the week were
  117866 tonnes vs 121168 tonnes a week ago and compared with
  84250 tonnes in the yearago week
      NSPA said the figures include only NSPA member firms
