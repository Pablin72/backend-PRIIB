DUMEZ UNIT HAS 94 PCT OF WESTBURNE WBI
  Dumez Investments I Inc said 947 pct
  of Westburne International Industries Ltds outstanding common
  shares have been deposited under its takeover bid
      It said it has received about 11070000 shares under its
  2250 dlrs per share offer which expired yesterday
      Dumez said it will proceed to acquire the remaining common
  shares to give it 100 pct ownership of Westburne
      Dumez is a private company owned jointly by Dumez SA
  and Unicorp Canada Corp
