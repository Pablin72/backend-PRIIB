TURKISH INFLATION INCREASES IN MARCH
  Inflation in Turkey was 37 pct in March
  compared with 17 pct in February and 13 pct in March 1986
  the State Statistics Institute said
      The annual rate rose to 347 pct in March compared with
  317 pct in February and 342 pct in March 1986 The
  governments target is to reduce inflation this year to an
  annual 20 pct
      The consumer price index base 197879 was 19573
  compared with 18868 in February and 14527 in March 1986
