HONG KONG BANKS RAISE PRIME ONEHALF POINT TO 65 PCT
  Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corp and
  Standard Chartered Bank raised their prime rate onehalf point
  to 65 pct effective Tuesday the Association of Banks said
      The association said in a statement deposit rates have also
  been increased by 14 to 12 percentage point
      The banks last announced an adjustment on February 28 when
  they raised the prime rate by one point to six pct
