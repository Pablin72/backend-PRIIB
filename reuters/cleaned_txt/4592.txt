SEAL ltSINC ACQUIRES ADEMCO LTD
  Seal Inc said it acquired
  Ademco Ltd a United Kingdom company which distributes mounting
  and laminating equipment and supplies for a combination of
  cash and stock valued at 26 mln dlrs based on current
  exchange rates
      Seal said it will pay up to an additional 13 mln dlrs
  contingent on the market price of Seals common on December 31
  1988 and on certain earnings targets by the acquired business
