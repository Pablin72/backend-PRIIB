GHANA COCOA PURCHASES SLOW
  The Ghana Cocoa Board said it purchased
  214 tonnes of cocoa in the 24th week ended March 19 of the
  198687 main crop season compared with 456 tonnes the previous
  week and 372 tonnes in the 24th week ended March 27 of the
  198586 season
      Cumulative purchases so far this season stand at 217449
  tonnes ahead of the 204256 tonnes purchased by the 24th week
  of last season
