MADAGASCAR COCOA PRODUCTION ESTIMATED HIGHER
  Madagascars cocoa production is
  estimated 13 pct higher this year at 2720 tonnes up from
  2400 in 1986 Agriculture Ministry officials said
      This improvement reflects the governments efforts over the
  last seven years to extend existing cocoa plantations and plant
  new higher yielding varieties particularly at the northern tip
  of the island they said
      Last year Madagascar exported 2189 tonnes of high quality
  cocoa up from 1624 in 1985 the Trade Ministry said
      This years exports are estimated at 2400 tonnes
