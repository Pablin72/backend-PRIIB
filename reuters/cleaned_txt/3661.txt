SPAINS MONEY SUPPLY GROWTH DOUBLES IN FEBRUARY
  Spains broad based M4 money supply
  rose at an annualised rate of 167 pct in February against 81
  pct in January and 224 pct in February last year Bank of
  Spain figures show
      The broadbased money supply is measured as liquid assets
  in public hands plus quasimonetary assets
      Money supply growth was 114 pct last year The government
  wants to reduce the rate to eight pct this year
