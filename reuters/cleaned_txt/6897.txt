TAIWAN BUYS 450000 TONNES OF US CORN
  Taiwan overnight bought 450000
  tonnes of US number two corn 145 pct moisture for Gulf and
  West Coast shipment in 11 cargoes between May and October
  private export sources said
      Gulf shipment ranged in prices from 7517 to 7845 dlrs per
  tonne stowed and trimmed FOB euqivalent and West Coast
  shipments ranged from 7941 to 8102 dlrs per tonne stowed and
  trimmed FOB equivalent they said
