US HOUSING COMPLETIONS FELL 02 PCT IN JANUARY
  US Completions of new homes fell
  02 pct in January to a seasonally adjusted rate of 1884 mln
  units from 1888 mln in December the Commerce Department said
      The January fall came after a strong 64 pct rise from
  Novembers rate of 1774 mln units and brought completions to
  67 pct above the January 1986 level of 1765 mln units
      In January completions of singlefamily units rose 04 pct
  to a seasonally adjusted 1183 mln units from 1178 mln units
  in December while multifamily units fell 13 pct to 701000
  units in January the department said
