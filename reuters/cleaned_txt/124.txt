US GRAIN CARLOADINGS FALL IN WEEK
  US grain carloadings totaled 26108
  cars in the week ended February 21 down 22 pct from the
  previous week but 228 pct above the corresponding week a year
  ago the Association of American Railroads reported
      Grain mill product loadings in the week totalled 11382
