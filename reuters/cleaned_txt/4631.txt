JAPAN SETS 198788 FIRST HALF BEEF IMPORT QUOTA
  The Agriculture Ministry said it set
  Japans beef import quota for the six months from April 1 at
  93000 tonnes up from 83000 in the second half of 198687 and
  85000 a year earlier
      Under an agreement with the US And Australia Japan has
  been increasing imports by 9000 tonnes a year from March 31
  1985 to reach a projected 177000 tonnes in the year to March
  31 1988
