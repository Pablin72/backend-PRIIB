DUTCH ADJUSTED UNEMPLOYMENT UNCHANGED IN FEBRUARY
  Dutch seasonallyadjusted
  unemployment totalled 690600 people in February unchanged
  from January but down from 732700 in February 1986 a Social
  Affairs Ministry spokesman said
      The unadjusted figure stood at 708700 at the end of last
  month a decline of 3800 from the January total of 712500 and
  comparing with 750000 at the end of February last year
