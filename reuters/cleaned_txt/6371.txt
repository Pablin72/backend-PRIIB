GERMAN PRODUCER PRICES FALL 03 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  West German producer prices fell 03
  pct in February from January and were 42 pct below prices in
  February last year the Federal Statistics Office said
      In January producer prices rose 02 pct from December the
  first monthly rise since October 1985 but they were down 44
  pct from a year earlier
      The Statistics Office said light heating oil producer
  prices fell 22 pct in February from January while heavy heating
  oil prices fell 24 pct
