SWISS WHOLESALE PRICES FALL 03 PCT IN FEBRUARY
  Swiss wholesale prices fell by 03 pct in
  February giving a drop of 43 pct from February 1986 the
  Federal Office of Industry Trade and Labour said
      Prices had been unchanged in January giving an annual drop
  of 46 pct They fell by 26 pct in the year to February 1986
      The wholesale price index base 1963 stood at 1685 in
  February from 1761 last year
      The office said the fall was largely due to lower prices
  for energy raw materials and semifinished goods Consumer
  goods prices rose slightly
