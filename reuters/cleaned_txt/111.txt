US M1 MONEY SUPPLY ROSE 21 BILLION DLRS
  US M1 money supply rose 21 billion
  dlrs to a seasonally adjusted 7367 billion dlrs in the
  February 16 week the Federal Reserve said
      The previous weeks M1 level was revised to 7346 billion
  dlrs from 7342 billion dlrs while the fourweek moving
  average of M1 rose to 7350 billion dlrs from 7335 billion
      Economists polled by Reuters said that M1 should be
  anywhere from down four billion dlrs to up 23 billion dlrs
  The average forecast called for a 300 mln dlr M1 rise
