SEDGWICK BUYS BSI INCORP
  Sedgwick Group Plc ltSDWKL said its
  whollyowned Canadian subsidiary Sedgwick Tomenson Inc had
  acquired BSi Incorp for a maximum eight mln Canadian dlrs
      The funds will be raised through the issue of up to 116
  mln ordinary Sedgwick shares to the vendors of BSi by no later
  than 31 January 1991 Some 427054 shares already have been
  issued
      BSi is a privatelyheld Canadian company involved in
  actuarial employee and executive benefits consulting and
  administration Its 1986 pretax profits excluding extraordinary
  items totalled 114 mln Canadian dlrs
