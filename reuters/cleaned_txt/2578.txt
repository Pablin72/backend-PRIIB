INSTRUMENTARIUM ACQUIRES NOKIA SUBSIDIARY
  Finlands medical group Instrumentarium
  Oy ltINMRHE said it has acquired electronics components
  importers and marketers ltFerrado Oy and ltInsele Oy
  subsidiaries of Finlands electronics group Nokia Oy ltNOKSHE
      It said in a statement Ferrado and Insele will be merged
  into Instrumentariums Professional Electronics and Information
  Systems Division
      It did not disclose a price for the acquisitions but said
  it had issued 30000 restricted B shares as partial payment to
  Nokia
