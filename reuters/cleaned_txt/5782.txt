THAI M1 MONEY SUPPLY RISES IN JANUARY
  Thailands M1 money supply rose 98
  pct to an estimated 1173 billion baht in January after
  increasing 66 pct in December the Bank of Thailand said
      It said yearonyear M1 grew 231 pct in January after an
  185 pct increase in December
      The central bank said M2 rose 17 pct to an estimated
  6865 billion baht in January after a 24 pct increase in
  December while yearonyear it expanded 133 pct after a 126
  pct rise in December
