CANADIAN MONEY SUPPLY FALLS WEEK Canadian narrowly defined money supply M-1 fell 555 mln dlrs 32.42 billion dlrs week ended March 18, Bank Canada said. M-1-A, M-1 daily interest chequable non-personal deposits, fell 559 mln dlrs 74.86 billion dlrs M-2, M-1-A notice personal fixed-term deposit fell 439 mln dlrs 177.30 billion dlrs. M-3, non-personal fixed term deposits foreign currency deposits residents booked chartered banks Canada, fell 696 mln dlrs 215.74 billion dlrs.
