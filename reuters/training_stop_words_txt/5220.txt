china reports 700 mln dlr two-month trade deficit china's trade deficit totalled 700 mln dlrs months year, figures released state statistics bureau. china news agency quoted bureau foreign trade totalled 9.3 billion dlrs period, exports worth 4.3 billion dlrs. bureau total trade volume 2.5 pct 1986 period, exports 18.1 pct, gave comparative figures. china's 1986 trade deficit totalled 12 billion dlrs, official figures show.
