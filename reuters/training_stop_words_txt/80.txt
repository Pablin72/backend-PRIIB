YORK BUSINESS LOANS FALL 195 MLN DLRS Commercial industrial loans books 10 major York banks, excluding acceptances, fell 195 mln dlrs 65.06 billion week ended February 18, Federal Reserve Bank York said. Including acceptances, loans declined 114 mln dlrs 65.89 billion. Commercial paper outstanding nationally dropped 375 mln dlrs 336.63 billion. National business loan data scheduled released Friday.
