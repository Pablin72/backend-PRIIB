GERMAN FEBRUARY IMPORT PRICES FALL Import prices West Germany fell 0.7 pct February January stand 15.6 pct level February 1986, Federal Statistics Office said. January import price index, base 1980, unchanged compared December 17.8 pct lower January 1986. February export prices, base import prices, unchanged compared January 2.5 pct lower February 1986. January export prices fell 0.3 pct December stand 3.0 pct lower January 1986.
