USDA ESTIMATES AUSTRALIA WHEAT CROP U.S. Agriculture Department forecast Australia's 1986/87 wheat crop 17.30 mln tonnes, 17.50 mln tonnes month. estimated 1985/86 output 16.13 mln tonnes, 16.13 mln month. Australian wheat exports 1986/87 forecast 14.50 mln tonnes, 15.00 mln tonnes month, exports 1985/86 estimated 15.96 mln tonnes, 15.96 mln month.
