JAPAN FEBRUARY MONEY SUPPLY RISES 8.8 PCT Japan's broadly defined money supply average M-2 certificate deposits (CDs) rose preliminary 8.8 pct February year earlier, compared 8.6 pct rise January, Bank Japan said. seasonally adjusted February average M-2 CDs supply rose 0.8 pct January, said.
