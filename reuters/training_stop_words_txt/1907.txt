AUSTRALIAN WHEAT EXPORTS RISE FEBRUARY Australian wheat shipments rose 1.33 mln tonnes February 1.01 mln January, Australian Wheat Board official said. February's shipments 1.54 mln tonnes shipped February 1986. Cumulative shipments months October/September wheat year 6.12 mln tonnes, 6.54 mln year earlier, official said. major destinations February were: China (419,196 tonnes), Egypt (301,909), Iraq (142,055), Japan (110,261), South Korea (100,847) Soviet Union (100,056 tonnes).
