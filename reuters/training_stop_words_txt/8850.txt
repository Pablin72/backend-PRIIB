GHANA COCOA PURCHASES AHEAD YEAR Ghana Cocoa Board purchased 456 tonnes cocoa 23rd week, ended March 12, 1986/87 main crop season, compared 684 tonnes previous week 784 tonnes 23rd week ended March 20 1985/86 season. Cumulative purchases season stand 217,235 tonnes, ahead 203,884 tonnes purchased 23rd week season, board said.
