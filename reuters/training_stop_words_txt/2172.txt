FRENCH CEREAL EXPORTS ROUEN FALL French cereal exports port Rouen fell 6.4 pct 725,023 tonnes period February 1 25, 774,704 period February 1 26 year, trade sources said. Main destinations Saudi Arabia 158,109 tonnes barley, Soviet Union 147,214 wheat, Italy 104,704 wheat, Spain 91,381 wheat maize, China 52,500 wheat Algeria 41,000 wheat. February 26 today, ships loaded 137,000 tonnes cereals, sources added. 137,000 tonnes comprised 59,500 tonnes wheat China, 53,000 wheat Soviet Union 24,500 wheat Algeria. Friday, sources estimated 233,600 tonnes cereals loaded, comprising 47,000 tonnes barley, 78,600 wheat 25,000 rapeseed Soviet Union, 30,000 wheat Sri Lanka, 28,000 wheat China 25,000 wheat Poland.
