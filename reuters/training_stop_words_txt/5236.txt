NORWAY'S TRADE DEFICIT WIDENS FEBRUARY Norway's trade deficit widened February 957 mln crowns 80 mln crowns January 492 mln crowns February year, Central Bureau Statistics said. Exports dropped 10.66 billion crowns month, compared 11.11 billion January 10.85 billion February 1986, added. Crude oil natural gas exports totalled 4.56 billion crowns February, 5.10 billion January 5.36 billion year ago.
