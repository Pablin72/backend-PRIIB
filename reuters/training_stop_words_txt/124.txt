U.S. GRAIN CARLOADINGS FALL WEEK U.S. grain carloadings totaled 26,108 cars week ended February 21, 2.2 pct previous week 22.8 pct week year ago, Association American Railroads reported. Grain mill product loadings week totalled 11,382 cars, 1.8 pct previous week 7.6 pct week year earlier, association said.
