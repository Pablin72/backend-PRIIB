CANADA RETAIL SALES FALL 0.1 PCT JANUARY Canada's retail sales, seasonally adjusted, fell 0.1 pct January gaining 0.9 pct December, Statistics Canada said. Retail sales fell 11.98 billion dlrs 11.99 billion dlrs December, 1986. Unadjusted sales 6.9 pct higher January, 1986. January, automobile sales fell 1.9 pct, department store sales slipped 3.2 pct variety stores sales plunged 14 pct. declines offset 5.5 pct increase grocery store sales 9.9 pct gain hardware sales.
