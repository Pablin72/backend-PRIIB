ASCS BUYS PROCESSED PRODUCTS DOMESTIC Agricultural Stabilization Conservation Service (ASCS) bought 25.7 mln pounds wheat flour cost 2.8 mln dlrs domestic shipment April 1-15 April 16-30, ASCS spokesman said. ASCS bought 11.1 mln pounds bakers flour 1.1 mln dlrs, 1.9 mln pounds pasta 408,258 dlrs, 1.4 mln pounds processed cereal products 304,043 dlrs, 4.8 mln pounds corn products 474,944 dlrs, 16.3 mln pounds milled rice 2.0 mln dlrs.
