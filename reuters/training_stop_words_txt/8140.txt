JAPANESE COMPOUND FEED OUTPUT FALLS JANUARY Japanese compound feed output fell 2.06 mln tonnes January 2.57 mln December, 2.04 mln year earlier, Agriculture Ministry statistics show. January sales totalled 2.07 mln tonnes 2.59 mln December 2.04 mln year earlier, end-month stocks 233,003 tonnes 230,764 241,567. Base mixes January compound feed output included corn, sorghum soybean meal. Corn totalled 993,156 tonnes, 1.20 mln December 896,718 year earlier, compounding ratio 48.1 pct 46.6 pct 43.1 pct. Sorghum totalled 339,013 tonnes January 459,067 December 412,743 year earlier, compounding ratio 16.4 pct 17.8 pct 19.8 pct, ministry's figures shows. Soybean meal amounted 202,546 tonnes 253,498 213,287 compounding ratio 9.8 pct 9.8 pct 10.2 pct.
