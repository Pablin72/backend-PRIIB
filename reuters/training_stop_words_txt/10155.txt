YORK BUSINESS LOANS FALL 222 MLN DLRS Commercial industrial loans books 10 major York banks, excluding acceptances, fell 222 mln dlrs 64.05 billion week ended March 18, Federal Reserve Bank York said. Including acceptances, loans fell 390 mln dlrs 64.74 billion. Commercial paper outstanding nationally rose 35 mln dlrs 339.04 billion. National business loan data scheduled released Friday.
