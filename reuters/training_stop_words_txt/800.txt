COPPER STOCKS U.S. INCREASE DECEMBER Refined copper stocks held refineries U.S. increased preliminary 277,500 short tons end December preliminary 270,000 short tons (revised higher) end November, American Bureau Metal Statistics said. London Metal Exchange stocks fell 193,100 tons December 194,400 tons November. Combined refinery LME stocks rose preliminary 470,600 tons 464,400 tons (revised higher). January LME stocks 193,400 tons. Preliminary figures showed refined production U.S. lower 377,300 tons December versus 384,000 tons November. Crude output decreased 421,500 tons 427,000 tons (revised higher). Refined copper deliveries decreased 359,800 tons 375,400 tons (revised lower).
