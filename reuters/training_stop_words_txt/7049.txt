ITALIAN NET RESERVES RISE FEBRUARY Italy's net official reserves rose 66,172 billion lire February 1987 previously reported 62,174 billion January, Bank Italy said. Gold holdings end-February totalled 35,203 billion lire, unchanged January. Convertible currencies totalled 18,467 billion lire, 14,899 billion January, European Currency Unit (ECU) holdings 10,156 billion lire 10,133 billion.
