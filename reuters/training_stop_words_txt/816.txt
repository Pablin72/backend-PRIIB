U.S. BRASS MILL COPPER STOCKS LOWER JANUARY U.S. brass mill copper stocks fell 185,400 short tons (copper content) end January 191,200 short tons end December, American Bureau Metal Statistics. Consumption brass mills increased 60,700 short tons January 48,900 short tons December. Mills consumed 29,600 tons refinery shapes January versus 24,000 tons December. Scrap consumption increased 31,100 tons January 24,900 tons December. Total brass mill shipments increased 69,600 tons January 54,400 tons December, receipts rose 63,800 tons 47,100 tons.
