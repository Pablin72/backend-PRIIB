GERMAN WHOLESALE PRICES FALL 0.4 PCT FEBRUARY West German wholesale prices fell 0.4 pct February January stand 6.9 pct lower February 1986, Federal Statistics Office said. January wholesale prices rose 0.8 pct December stand 8.6 pct level January 1986. Wholesale price declines led starch products, 13 pct, light heating oil 11 pct lower, heavy heating oil 10 pct lower green coffee 6.9 pct. higher wholesale prices fresh vegetables, 17 pct, fresh fruit 12 pct eggs 8.9 pct, statistics office statement.
