ARGENTINE OIL PRODUCTION JANUARY 1987 Argentine crude oil production 10.8 pct January 1987 12.32 mln barrels, 13.81 mln barrels January 1986, Yacimientos Petroliferos Fiscales said. January 1987 natural gas output totalled 1.15 billion cubic metrers, 3.6 pct higher 1.11 billion cubic metres produced January 1986, Yacimientos Petroliferos Fiscales added.
