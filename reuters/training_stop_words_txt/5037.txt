U.S. PRODUCER ENERGY PRICES RISE FEBRUARY Prices wholesale finished energy goods U.S. rose 4.0 pct February 9.8 pct rise January, Labor Department said. Producer Price Index finished energy goods fell 20.9 pct past 12 months. Heating oil prices rose 3.0 pct February 18.0 pct rise January, department said. Gasoline prices rose 5.5 pct month 15.7 pct January rise, department said. Natural gas prices rose 1.8 pct 4.2 pct rise January. Crude oil prices rose 4.4 pct February, 19.7 pct January rise 21.3 pct year ago level.
