U.S. REFINERY COPPER STOCKS FALL JANUARY Refined copper stocks held U.S. refineries fell 109,200 short tons end January 145,400 short tons end December, American Bureau Metal Statistics reported. Commodity Exchange (Comex) copper stocks climbed 103,000 tons January 93,300 tons December. Combined refinery Comex stocks eased 212,200 tons January 238,700 tons December. U.S. refined production declined 117,600 tons January 137,400 tons December. Crude output increased 98,600 tons January 93,300 tons (revised lower) December. Preliminary figures showed U.S. refined deliveries rose 151,800 tons January 124,800 tons December.
