GHANA COCOA PURCHASES FALL, CUMULATIVE Ghana Cocoa Board purchased 1,323 tonnes cocoa 21st week, ended February 26, 1986/87 main crop season, compared 1,961 tonnes previous week 1,344 tonnes 21st week ended March 1985/86 season, board said. Cumulative purchases season stand 216,095 tonnes, 201,966 tonnes purchased 21st week season, Board said.
